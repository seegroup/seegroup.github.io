---
title: SEE Research Group receives Vinnova grant for Human-AI Collaboration project
---

We are thrilled to announce that the SEE Research Group has been awarded **SEK 3,162,000** in funding from **Vinnova** for the project *"Decision Aware Human AI Collaboration for Industrial Digital Products"* (ref. 2026-00111), running from April 2026 to April 2028. The project is led by **Sergio Rico**, with **Awais Ahmad** and **Felix Dobslaw** as key members, and is carried out in collaboration with **Volvo Trucks**. This grant is a fantastic recognition of our work in applied AI and a great opportunity to make a real industrial impact — we couldn't be more excited!
