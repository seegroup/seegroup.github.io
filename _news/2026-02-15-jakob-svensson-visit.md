![Prof. Jakob Svensson during his visit to the SEE research group]({{ '/assets/images/jakob-visit-2026.png' | relative_url }})

Last week we welcomed **Prof. Jakob Svensson** (Malmö University) to the SEE research group in Östersund. Jakob conducted interviews with software engineers at our partner organisations as part of a study on emotions when using Generative AI in software development, gave a well-received talk on **Digital Ageism** in the software sector, and shared a preview of his current work on *Gomorron Östersund*.
