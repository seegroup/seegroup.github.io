---
title: Paper accepted in ACM TOSEM — special issue on Software Engineering 2030
---

Our paper **"Challenges in Testing Large Language Model Based Software: A Faceted Taxonomy"** by Felix Dobslaw, Robert Feldt (Chalmers University of Technology), Juyeon Yoon, and Shin Yoo (KAIST, Republic of Korea) has been accepted in the ACM *Transactions on Software Engineering and Methodology* (TOSEM) special issue on Software Engineering 2030. The paper presents a taxonomy for LLM test case design, addressing the non-determinism of LLM-based software and introducing the notion of correctness as a distribution of outcomes rather than a binary property.
