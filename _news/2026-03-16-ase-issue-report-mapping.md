---
title: New ASE journal paper on automatic issue report classification
---

A new paper by **Laiq Muhammad** and **Felix Dobslaw** has been accepted to the *Automated Software Engineering* journal, presenting a systematic mapping of 46 studies on automatic techniques for classifying software issue reports into bugs and non-bugs. The study shows how approaches ranging from traditional machine learning to deep learning and recent LLMs still rarely involve practitioners or real industrial evaluations, highlighting important directions for future research.
