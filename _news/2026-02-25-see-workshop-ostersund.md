---
title: SEE research group workshop on campus Östersund
---

On **23–24 February 2026** we held a **SEE research group workshop** on campus Östersund, where many of us in the growing group met in person for the first time. We welcomed, among others, our new guest professor **Torgeir**, played curling together, and ran an academic \"speed dating\" session to get to know each other’s research interests and future collaboration ideas.
