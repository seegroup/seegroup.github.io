---
title: Participation in Nordic software engineering unconference in Kvitfjell
---

From **9–11 March 2026**, three of us from Mid Sweden University participated in a Nordic software engineering unconference in **Kvitfjell, Norway**. It was a fantastic location with great hospitality from the organizers, and Felix gave a lightning talk about collaboration in the Nordics while we gathered inspiration for a future Swedish unconference in the coming years.
