title: Three recent paper acceptances in 2026
---

Our group is happy to share that three works have recently been accepted:

- Addressing Trust Requirements in the Design of an Open-Source Multi-Agent LLM-Based Domain-Specific Chatbot – accepted for publication in **Requirements Engineering (Springer Nature)**.
- The Expert Validation Framework (EVF): Enabling Domain Expert Control in AI Engineering – accepted to the **CAIN 2026 Industry Track (ICSE Companion ’26)**, with a preprint available on [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12327).
- Understanding on the Edge: LLM-generated Boundary Test Explanations – accepted to the **7th ACM/IEEE International Conference on Automation of Software Test (AST 2026)**.
