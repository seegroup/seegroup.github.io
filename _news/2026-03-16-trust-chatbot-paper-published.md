---
title: Trust requirements chatbot paper now published in Requirements Engineering
---

The paper **“Addressing Trust Requirements in the Design of an Open-Source Multi-Agent LLM-Based Domain-Specific Chatbot”** by Jonatan Axetorn, Felix Edholm, Felix Dobslaw, and Lucas Gren is now published open access in *Requirements Engineering (Springer)*, presenting a three-agent architecture (judge, generator, checker) for an open-source HR chatbot focused on engineering trust from the ground up. For a short overview and context, see [Lucas Gren’s LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/lucasgren_addressing-trust-requirements-in-the-design-activity-7439275045685850112-PbZ1?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAAFqLL8BU05AMpeSueG7sPm5kIznutP1DOU).
